## FAQ

### 1. What does Topify actually track?

Topify tracks how your brand appears across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview, including visibility, position, and sentiment for relevant prompts.

### 2. Who is Topify built for?

Topify fits in-house marketing teams, agencies, and growth-focused startups, but this page is tailored to larger SaaS teams that need repeatable GEO reporting and action.

### 3. How is GEO different from SEO?

SEO focuses on rankings in traditional search results. GEO focuses on whether AI engines mention, cite, and recommend your brand inside generated answers.

### 4. Can we track prompts specific to our category?

Yes. Topify supports custom prompts in addition to system-generated prompts, so teams can monitor the questions that matter most to their market.

### 5. Does Topify only report, or does it suggest actions too?

It does both. Topify shows AI visibility data and generates recommendations your team can review and approve to create GEO-focused content faster.

### 6. How quickly can an enterprise team get value?

Topify positions onboarding as fast and lightweight. Teams can start benchmarking AI visibility quickly without a heavy implementation project or complex setup.
