Frequently asked questions

What does Topify track?

It tracks how your brand appears across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview, including visibility, position, and sentiment.

Yes. Topify supports custom prompts in addition to system-generated prompts so teams can monitor category-specific questions.
